Classification App

This app will utilize a consumer dataset as in the coffee app and categorize consumers according to their buying behavior.
A use of categorized and classified customers will be planning of inventory to cater specifically to the needs of the groups of customers frequenting a certain store location or to plan marketing interventions towards strategies that are proven effective in a certain type of the target audience.
To achieve this, I will employ a mix of unsupervised classification methods (based on K) and a time series based analysis of reaction to a marketing relevant decision variable.
A working version of this app will be published soon.

Development goals

  • Visual representation of consumer behaviour
  • Various categorization models
  • Visualization of each model
  • Display of marketing variables
  • Analysis of reaction to a change in marketing variables
  • Visualization of the reaction for each consumer group
