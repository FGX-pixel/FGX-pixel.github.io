Meteorite App

An R-based web app to visualize meteorite crashes.
This Web app imports data from the NASA data portal then prepares a set of well-documented meteor crashes. A slider allows you to choose a starting year and an end year for a timeframe. The crashes will be filtered to fit into the chosen timeframe and are displayed on a world map.
Additional parts of the dashboard are a box to count the number of crashes in the selected timeframe and a histogram plot to visualize the weight distribution of the meteorites.

If you play with the selection of the start and end of the timeframe you will notice that in earlier years only comparably heavy meteorites were observed. Also, most observations take place from 1900.
