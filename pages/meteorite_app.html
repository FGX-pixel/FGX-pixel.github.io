Meteorite App

An R-based webapp to visualize meteorite crashes.
This Webapp imports data from the NASA Data portal, then prepares a set of well documented meteor crashes. A slider allows you to choose a starting year and an end year for a timeframe. the crashes will be filtered to fit into the chosen timeframe and are displayed on a world map.
part of the dashboard is as well a box to count the number of crashes in the selected timeframe and a histogram plot to visualize weight distribution of the meteors.

If you play with the selection of the start and end of the timeframe you will notice that in earlier years only comparably heavy meteorites were observed. Also most observations take place from 1900.
